English Learning Path: A1 to C1

Introduction

Welcome to your English learning journey. This guide will help you progress from A1 to C1 level, covering essential themes and vocabulary for each level. Whether you're just starting or aiming to refine your skills, this resource is designed for adult learners looking for a structured path.

Levels Overview

A1 - Beginner

Topics:

  • Greetings and Introductions
  • Numbers and Dates
  • Daily Routines
  • Simple Descriptions

Grammar:

  • Present Simple Tense
  • Subject Pronouns
  • Articles (a, an, the)
  • Basic Questions

Vocabulary:

  • Common Verbs
  • Basic Nouns
  • Adjectives for Descriptions
  • Useful Phrases

A2 - Elementary

Topics:

  • Everyday Activities
  • Shopping and Food
  • Travel and Transportation
  • Health and Body

Grammar:

  • Present Continuous Tense
  • Comparatives and Superlatives
  • Simple Past Tense
  • Future Forms

Vocabulary:

  • Food and Drink
  • Clothing
  • Travel Terms
  • Common Adjectives

B1 - Intermediate

Topics:

  • Personal Experiences
  • Work and Study
  • Hobbies and Leisure
  • Shopping and Services

Grammar:

  • Past Continuous Tense
  • Present Perfect Tense
  • Conditionals (Type 1)
  • Comparative Structures

Vocabulary:

  • Work-Related Terms
  • Hobbies and Activities
  • Travel and Accommodation
  • Feelings and Emotions

B2 - Upper Intermediate

Topics:

  • Current Events
  • Debates and Opinions
  • Media and Culture
  • Advanced Travel

Grammar:

  • Past Perfect Tense
  • Future Perfect Tense
  • Conditionals (Type 2 and 3)
  • Modals of Deduction

Vocabulary:

  • Idiomatic Expressions
  • Formal and Informal Language
  • Advanced Phrasal Verbs
  • Complex Sentences

C1 - Advanced

Topics:

  • Abstract Concepts
  • Academic Writing
  • Professional Communication
  • Advanced Discussions

Grammar:

  • Mixed Conditionals
  • Complex Sentence Structures
  • Advanced Passive Voice
  • Subjunctive Mood

Vocabulary:

  • Advanced Vocabulary and Collocations
  • Formal Register
  • Specialized Terminology
  • Nuanced Meanings
